this is
Our Story

OnePwaa was born from the passion
of a group of scholars around the world.

OnePwaa is an international nonprofit dedicated to improving access to education among schoolchildren. Our mandate is to advocate for the concept and practice of collaborative learning principally through the World Scholar’s Cup (WSC) and other educational programs. We offer resources and services including but not limited to scholarship programs, debate camps, and study guides. We pledge to reinvest all profits obtained into the education of underprivileged communities internationally.

We pledge to offer our resources and services to all people—regardless of ethnicity, race, religion, socioeconomic status, gender identity and sexual orientation—undoubtedly committed to reducing systemic disadvantages in education.
